# Argumentative essay

## Thesis

Thesis: Community service should be required for graduation. Helping communities can improve a teen's mentality and create productive members of society.

  - Claim: Community service should be required for graduation.
  - Reason: Helping communities can improve a teen's mentality and create productive members of society.
    1. Helping communities
    2. Improving a teen's mentality
    3. Creating productive members of society

## Conclusion

2nd to last paragraph:

  * Counterclaim
    - Transition word/phrase that shows contrast
  * Explanation of that side
    - Fairly discuss the other side
  * Rebuttal
    - Explain why that (the counterclaim) is wrong and your argument is the
      "right" one.

Last Paragraph &mdash; Conclusion:

  * Connect your warrant to your evidence (summarize your body paragraphs)
  * Restate counterclaim
    - Use different, more basic terms. (Summary of counterclaim paragraph)
  * Draw conclusion to restate your thesis/claim. (Summarize 1st paragraph)
