Assignment
  1. Use available resources to locate five of the designers' current fashions for the fall/winter or spring/summer collections.
  2. Find at least one matching design from history that you imagine might have inspired the current designer.
  3. Create a side-by-side visual comparison of each of the five current designs with those from history.
  4. Compare the designs by including:
    • How the designs are similar
    • How the designs are different
    • What changes would you make to the design? Why?

You may use Canva or Google Slides. Limit your slides to 5 (1 per designer). Use your own words and pay attention to grammar and spelling.
