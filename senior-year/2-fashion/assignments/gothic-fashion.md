# Early Fashion #4

Investigate the general fashion from Gothic civilization/era. Use google or canva for your assignment and include the following information:
- Types of fashion for males, females, and children
- Fiber choices for those fashions
- Importance of fashion in that era
- Influences on fashion

The assignment should include pictures of the fashion during the civilization or era. Limit your slides to 3.

## Info

Gothic fashion was a marginally dark or monochrome style of clothing that often utilized floral patterns.
